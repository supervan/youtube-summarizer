Getting Started

1

Paste URL

Copy links from YouTube videos and paste them into the input box. We support both regular video links and Shorts.

2

Customize

Choose your preferred summary Length (Short, Medium, Long) and Tone (Conversational, Professional, Witty, etc.) to get exactly what you need.

3

Generate

Click "Let's Go" and wait a few seconds. Our AI will analyze the video captions and produce a concise summary for you.

Features

AI Summary

Get instant summaries broken down by key points. Perfect for long tutorials, lectures, or news updates.

AI Chat

Have specific questions? Chat with the video content to find exactly what you're looking for without watching.

Quiz Mode

Test your understanding with a 5-question interactive quiz. New: Instantly refresh questions if you want to try again!

Read Aloud & Podcast

Listen to the summary on the go with our realistic text-to-speech voices, or generate a mini-podcast.

Step-by-Step Guides

Extract actionable tutorials and instructions. Steps are automatically saved to your history so you can come back to them later.

Mind Map

Visualize the video's concepts in an interactive graph found in the "Chat" tab.
