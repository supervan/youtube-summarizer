Introducing SuperVan Speed Test: Professional Grade Diagnostics

We are excited to launch our second major tool: SuperVan Speed Test, a professional-grade internet diagnostic tool designed for accuracy, privacy, and ease of use.

Why Another Speed Test?

Most internet speed tests are cluttered with ads, slow to load, or track excessive amounts of user data. We wanted to build something different: a tool that is lightweight, beautiful, and privacy-focused.

Key Features

  • Detailed Metrics: Measure Download, Upload, and Ping latency with high precision.
  • Historical Logging: Automatically save your test history locally in your browser. Track your ISP's performance over time.
  • Privacy First: No account required. We don't store your personal IP address on our servers.
  • Dark Mode UI: A sleek, modern interface that looks great on any device.

Technical Behind the Scenes

The app is built using React and Vite for blazing fast performance. It utilizes modern Web Workers to ensure the UI remains responsive even while saturating your network connection during tests.
